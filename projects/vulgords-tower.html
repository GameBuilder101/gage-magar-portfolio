    Description

    Vulgord's tower is a turn-based, multiplayer, RPG, tabletop simulator where players race against each other to navigate the four realms and defeat the final boss. My roles in the project included programming, gameplay design, and 3D art. It was designed in collaboration with the game's other developer, who handled the remaining aspects.

    Process & Challenges

    This project was my introduction to the Unity engine and gave me experience in using networking APIs to handle online multiplayer through the Steam P2P system. It also served as a full exploration of the development process, from the prototyping phase to publishing.
