    Hello! My name is Gage Magar. I am a student at the Rochester Institute of Technology pursuing a degree in Game Design and Development. Most of my work includes personal and academic video game projects. I also have experience with web development from my time at globalHMA, a marketing agency, as well as from freelance work and a web development course.
